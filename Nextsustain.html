Solar energy harnesses the power of sunlight through photovoltaic panels to generate electricity.
It's a renewable and abundant resource that produces clean energy, emitting no greenhouse gases or pollutants during operation. Solar panels can be installed on rooftops, in solar farms, or integrated into various structures, providing a decentralized energy solution. Advancements in solar technology have made it more efficient and cost-effective over the years, contributing to its widespread adoption in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.
